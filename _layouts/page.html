MyBB 1.6 Series EOL – Important Notice

The MyBB 1.6 series reached End-Of-Life on October 1, 2015. The MyBB Group strongly encourages all communities running MyBB 1.6 to upgrade as soon as possible to avoid the possibility of future bugs and/or vulnerabilities. Find out more →

The 1.6 Documentation is no longer maintained and some information may be outdated.
